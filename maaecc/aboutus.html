Company Overview

The MAA CONSTRUCTION & ENGINEERING COMPANY was conceived in the passionate dream to construct value standing on the strong foundation of quality, transparency and trust. Maa Construction & Engineering Company, took the shape of reality in the year 2012. The journey of a thousand miles began with this single step and in the course of time achieved many milestones in its journey and set benchmarks and standards for others to emulate.


MAA CONSTRUCTION & ENGINEERING COMPANY is lead by a visionary and passionate Person Mr. V. Srinivas Goud. His commitment to quality and passion for perfection has helped Maa build a strong foundation of trust and win confidence of not only customers but also the stake holders in the construction fraternity. He brings to the company his rich experience and expertise of more than a decade in the construction industry. His exposure in working with big giants in the construction industry has not only sharpened his business acumen but also added value to the company's success.


MAA’ phenomenal success is driven by Mr. Srinivas Goud’s innovative and dedicated approach to each and every project he undertakes. His commitment towards perfection and delivering value to the client's satisfaction has helped him carve a niche in the construction industry.
