Company Policies

QUALITY POLICY

We at MAA CONSTRUCTION & ENGINEERING CO (HYD) focus on the quality construction of buildings, industrial structure, logistic and infrastructure in chosen markets. We are committed to earn the trust of all our achieve customer satisfaction. The effectiveness in compliance, and continual improvement of our quality system shall be driven by the process approach, we strive for optimal utilization of resources to complete project within budget and schedule.

EMPLOYEE HEALTH AND SAFETY POLICY

We at MAA CONSTRUCTION & ENGINEERING CO (HYD) committed to continually improving our performance by periodically evaluating Health and Safety Risks associated with construction activities, setting and reviewing Objectives and targets, Complying applicable legal and other requirements. Also committed to maintain safe and comfortable work environment to prevent or mitigate Physical injuries, Work related illness and Damage to property. We provide necessary information, instruction and training for those working for us, to make them aware of Health and Safety obligations. Managing Director.

Commitment to Quality & Dedication to Perfection

This policy recognizes that the Safe well-being of all personnel working for MAA CONSTRUCTION & ENGINEERING CO (HYD) is the responsibility of MAA Management. In fulfilling this responsibility, MAA Management realizes it has a duty to provide and maintain, so far as is reasonable & practicable, a working environment that is without risk to the health of any MAA personnel.

This responsibility includes:

  • Providing and maintaining safe plant and systems of work.
  • Making and monitoring arrangements for the safe use, handling, storage and Transport of plant and substances.
  • Maintaining the workplace in a safe and healthy condition and Providing information, training and supervision for all employees enabling them to work in a safe, conscientious manner.

MAA is totally committed to ensuring that its safety policy and procedures meets with all of the relevant regulatory, heritage and statutory requirements and through its policy of continual improvement it will endeavor to improve its performance in the prevention of workplace near miss, incidents and accidents. The Company Directors' are responsible for the implementation and monitoring of safety procedures and this policy. The safety duties of management at all levels will be detailed and company procedures for training and back-up support will be followed. In fulfilling the objective of this policy, management is committed to regular consultation with employees to ensure that the policy operates effectively, and that, health and safety issues are regularly reviewed.
